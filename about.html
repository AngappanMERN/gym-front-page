Our Story

Twelve years of putting coaching first

Gutim started in 2014 as a single room with a rack of dumbbells and a whiteboard. The idea hasn't changed since: give people a real program, correct their form, and check in on their progress. No fads, no gimmicks — just training that works and coaches who notice when you stop showing up.

Today we run strength, conditioning, mobility and recovery programs for members ranging from complete beginners to competitive athletes, all under one roof.
